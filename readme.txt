Reading post content out loud.

== Description ==

Text to Speech (also abbreviated as TTS) is a form of assistive technology that can read digital texts out loud. It is commonly known as read-aloud technology. The biggest attraction of this technology is that it is really simple to use. With a single touch of a finger, you will be able to convert all the text words on a website into audio and on any device such as smartphones, laptops, desktops, and tablets.
Wiloke AI Text To Speech is a WordPress plugin applies this technology.
